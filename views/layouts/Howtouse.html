The basics

So you've got a big project coming up and are totally overwhelmed by all the tiny little things that have to get done in an impossibly small amount of time. It's okay, we've all been there. And Dynamic Planner is about to make it easier. With the ability to zoom in on each day of the week and plan your project out by the hour, [IMAGE]you can break each humongous task into easy bite-sized steps. When a task is complete you simply click and drag it to the complete folder and watch it disappear![IMAGE] And with our handy progress bar you can track your progress each and every day until the project is complete.[IMAGE]
